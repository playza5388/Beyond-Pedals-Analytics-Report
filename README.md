# Beyond-Pedals-Analytics-Report
## Project background
### This project aims to analyze the key factors affecting Beyond Pedals' sales from January 2023 to September 2024, focusing on variables such as country, product category, seasonality, and discounts.
Founded in 2015 in South Africa, Beyond Pedals offers a diverse range of bicycles, including electric, mountain, and city bikes, serving customers in South Africa and Australia. The company blends innovation, quality, and sustainability to provide high-performance bikes for riders of all levels, promoting an active, eco-friendly lifestyle.

## Insights Deep-Dive
### Sales Trends by Country
- Australia outperforms South Africa in total sales, accounts for **57.44%** of overall sales, equating to **$158 million**.
- Sales in Australia and South Africa show strong seasonality, peaking between **April and June** and declining significantly from **July to December**.
- **March to April Sales Increase**:  
  - **Australia**: Sales grew by **193.2%** (2023) and **172.9%** (2024), demonstrating consistent seasonal growth.  
  - **South Africa**: Sales increased by **176.5%** (2023) and **145.5%** (2024), maintaining a similar seasonal pattern.

### Sales Trends by Product Category
- Sales are seasonal across product categories, with TOU leading at **39.73%** ($103M). Followed by ROB with **27.56%** the ORB with 25.21%.
- The least popular category is TRE, making up only **0.56%** ($1.4M) of total sales.
- In **April** of both years, sales across multiple product categories experienced significant growth, with **TRE** jumping by **1121%** (2023) and **EBI** rising by **1062%** (2024), marking a notable seasonal peak.
- In the quieter month of **December 2023**, sales distribution is more balanced across categories: TOU leads with **29.32%**, followed by ORB at **28.02%**, ROB at **22.48%**, and EBI at **18.80%**.
- In slower months, there are fewer extreme spikes or dips in demand for specific products, which results in a more stable share of total sales among the categories.
![Dashboard 2 (2)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a6884916-0790-41f4-b5a5-1682dad183b7)

### Sales Trends Across Discount Percentage Ranges
- The most effective discount range is **3-4%**, generating **69.17%** ($180M) of total sales. This is followed by discounts **over 5%**, which contribute **18.49%** ($48M) of sales.
- Sales generated by each discount category also show **seasonal variation**.
- In months where overall sales are low across all discount categories, even higher discounts (above 5%) do not significantly boost sales volume.
- Sales across all discount percentage ranges saw a significant increase in **April** during both years.

## Recommendations
### For seasonal sales management:
- Implement strategic discounts to address seasonal sales fluctuations, particularly during December and January when sales typically reach their lowest point
- Offer discounts above 5% during low-sales months to stimulate demand and increase sales volume

### For product category management:
- Phase out the underperforming product category TRE, which currently accounts for only 56% of total sales
- Specifically discontinue the FXGR1000 product line, which showed extremely low performance with just 105 units sold across 25 transactions in 2023
