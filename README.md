# Beyond-Pedals-Analytics-Report
### This project aims to analyze the key factors affecting Beyond Pedals' sales from January 2023 to September 2024, focusing on variables such as country, product category, seasonality, and discounts.
Founded in 2015 in South Africa, Beyond Pedals offers a diverse range of bicycles, including electric, mountain, and city bikes, serving customers in South Africa and Australia. The company blends innovation, quality, and sustainability to provide high-performance bikes for riders of all levels, promoting an active, eco-friendly lifestyle.

## Dashboard
The dashboard can be found in Tableau Public [here](https://public.tableau.com/views/Beyond-Pedals-Dashboard/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)
. This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends for Beyond Pedals from January 2023 to September 2024. It showcases quarterly sales performance, overall sales patterns, and a detailed monthly breakdown by country, product category, and discount percentage.
